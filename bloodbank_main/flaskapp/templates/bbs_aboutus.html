About Us

Welcome to the Blood Bank System web application, developed by a team of dedicated students from SRM University. Our application aims to revolutionize the process of blood donation and distribution by providing a comprehensive platform for donors, receivers, hospitals, and blood banks.


Key Features :


  • Donor and Receiver Registration : Users can register as donors or receivers to participate in blood donation and request processes.
  • Blood Donation Requests : Receivers can post requests for specific blood types, and donors can respond to these requests by donating blood directly or through blood banks.
  • Inventory Management : Hospitals and blood banks have access to inventory management tools to update and maintain their available stock of blood units.
  • Blood Community : A special community feature allows receivers to post urgent blood requests, which can be seen by both donors and blood banks.
  • User Profiles : Each user has a profile where they can manage their personal information, donation history, and donation preferences.
  • Search and Filter : Users can search for blood donors, blood banks, and hospitals based on location, blood type, and availability.
  • Notifications : Users receive notifications for donation requests, inventory updates, and other relevant activities.

Our team is committed to continually improving and expanding the features of the Blood Bank System to better serve the community and save lives.
